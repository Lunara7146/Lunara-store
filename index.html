📑 Terms & Conditions

1. Returns & Exchanges

Returns are only accepted if the product is defective due to manufacturer error (e.g., faulty stitching, incorrect sewing, or misprinted imagery). Customers must submit clear photos and physical evidence showing the defect as it arrived in the original packaging. Returns for reasons of personal preference (e.g., not liking the style, color, or fit) will not be accepted and will not qualify for credit.

2. Store Credit

Approved returns will be issued as Lunara Credits — store credit that can be used for discounts on future purchases. Cash refunds are not provided. Credits are only granted when manufacturer defects are verified. Credits cannot be claimed for items damaged after delivery by the customer.

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, customers must:

  • Submit photos of the defect within 7 days of delivery.
  • Provide evidence that the defect was present upon arrival (e.g., packaging photos).
  • Ship the item back in its original condition and packaging.

Returns that do not meet these requirements will be rejected.
